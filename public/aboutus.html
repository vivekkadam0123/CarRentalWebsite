Our Mission

India is urbanizing at a faster rate than any country in human history. As crores of individuals migrate to cities over the next decade, it will be imperative to create sustainable mobility solutions for people to get around their city.

About Us

WroomCar Pvt. Ltd. is a car rental company that rents a car to the user to fulfill his travelling needs. WroomCar was started in 2010 in Mumbai with 5 cars and today the company has 1000+ cars. We exist for a simple reason: we want to make renting easy andwe want to drive India's transformation to sustainable transportation. No more worries about petrol insurance, and car breakdowns! WroomCar has enabled driving convenience for travellers around the country and is fast expanding its reach to various cities. Self drive cars from WroomCar have given customers more control, privacy, and freedom. Book a self drive car in any city you visit with the WroomCar.
